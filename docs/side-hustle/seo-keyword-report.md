## General Marketplace Insights

### Gumroad Discover Search Dynamics
- **Gumroad Discover is the primary internal discovery engine** ‚Äî product titles, descriptions, and tags directly influence search ranking
- The marketplace is **heavily saturated** in generic AI/ChatGPT prompt categories (768+ results for "ChatGPT prompts bundle", 1143+ for "AI prompts marketing")
- **Niche-specific products outperform generic bundles** in conversion rate despite lower traffic
- Mega-bundles (100K+ prompts) dominate by volume but have low perceived quality ‚Äî opportunity exists for curated, specific toolkits
- **Free/PWYW products** are the #1 lead generation strategy on Gumroad (Interactive Book of Prompting: 108K+ downloads, 2,332 ratings)
- Products with the word "Playbook", "Toolkit", "System", or "Blueprint" in titles consistently appear in top results across categories

### Key Gumroad SEO Factors
1. **Product title** ‚Äî most important ranking signal on Discover
2. **Tags** ‚Äî Gumroad allows tagging; top tags in our space: `ai`, `chatgpt`, `chatgpt prompts`, `marketing`, `seo`, `productivity`
3. **Description keywords** ‚Äî indexed for search
4. **Sales velocity + ratings** ‚Äî social proof boosts rankings
5. **Price positioning** ‚Äî free/PWYW products get massively more visibility

### Category Placement
Most relevant Gumroad categories for our products:
- **Business & Money > Marketing & Sales** (for SEO, marketing, email products)
- **Business & Money > Entrepreneurship** (for solopreneur, launch products)
- **Software Development > Programming** (for developer, vibe coding products)
- **Self Improvement > Productivity** (for AI workflow products)
