## What This Is NOT

- **Not a course.** It's a newsletter. Short, useful, weekly.
- **Not a sales email.** Products are mentioned naturally, never the focus.
- **Not an AI news aggregator.** One highlight, curated and opinionated. Not a list of everything that happened.
