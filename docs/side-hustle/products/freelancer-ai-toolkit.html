Prompt 01 ‚Äî The Tailored Proposal

Prompt:

Why It Works: Clients skim proposals. This prompt forces structure ‚Äî problem recognition, phased approach, proof of competence, and a clear next step ‚Äî which mirrors how winning proposals are written by top freelancers. The word limit keeps it tight.

Example Output:

Prompt 08 ‚Äî The Scope Clarifier

Prompt:

Why It Works: Vague briefs create scope creep. Asking the right questions early positions you as thorough and experienced ‚Äî and protects both sides from mid-project surprises.

Example Output:

  2. Who’s the primary audience? Any personas or customer profiles I should review?
  3. Are there any existing brand assets (logos, fonts, colour codes, style guides) I should work with?
  4. What’s the ideal launch date? Is there a hard deadline or is there some flexibility?
  5. Do you have a budget range in mind? Even a ballpark helps me tailor the approach.
  6. Who’s the final decision-maker on approvals? Will I be working with you directly or is there a wider team?
  7. Are there any examples of competitors or brands whose approach you admire?

Happy to jump on a quick call if that’s easier ‚Äî otherwise, bullet-point answers are perfect.

Cheers, Taylor
