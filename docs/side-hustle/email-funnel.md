## Key Principles

1. **Give first, sell second.** Every email delivers standalone value even if they never buy.
2. **Pitch one product per email** (except Email 4 which shows the catalogue). Multiple CTAs kill conversions.
3. **The PS is prime real estate.** Use it for a personal note, a reply prompt, or a subtle secondary CTA.
4. **Reply prompts build trust.** Asking "what are you working on?" turns subscribers into relationships and gives you market intel.
5. **Don't discount.** These are already cheap. Discounting trains people to wait for sales. Instead, add value (bonus content, exclusive access).
